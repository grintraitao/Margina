Small status or count indicator. Used for "Beta", category counts, price-change deltas, and network status.

```jsx
<Badge tone="success" dot>Secure</Badge>
<Badge tone="primary" variant="solid">12</Badge>
<Badge tone="danger" variant="soft">-2.4%</Badge>
<Badge tone="violet" variant="outline">Beta</Badge>
```

Tones: `primary | success | warning | danger | violet | neutral`. Variants: `solid | soft | outline`. Optional leading `dot`.
